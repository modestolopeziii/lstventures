Features

Password Protection

Users are required to enter the correct user name and password before they can use the program.

Ease of Use

Our user-friendly Windows driven menu sequence makes it easy to learn and use the program. From the Menu bar, start from Left to Right; Top to bottom.

Updateable Government Tables

The program allows the users to add or change the government tables as required. The client may also be provided with an updated government table file which they can download to the program.

Define your Own Codes

You may enter as many codes (allowance, earnings, deductions, leaves … etc.) as you require. No unnecessary codes in your master file.

Multi-Department, Multi-Group System

The program allows you to group your employees. You can group them by payroll frequency (weekly, semi-monthly, monthly), by department or by branch.

Flexible Computation Set-Up

SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-Ibig Contributions.You can set the system to automatically deduct contributions based on monthly compensation or set it at a fixed monthly deduction.

Tax Computations.Able to set taxable and nontaxable income.

Able to consider taxable benefits in excess of P30,000.

Overtime Rates

Default overtime rates provided. Accomodates user defined rates.

ECOLA Computation

Automatically computes ECOLA based on the number of days worked.

Employee Default Set-Up

You may declare the necessary allowances, deductions or loans of an employee during your set-up so that the program will automatically add/deduct during payroll processing.

Monthly – Daily Employee & Conversion Rate

This feature defines how monthly rate is converted to daily rate. You may define one conversion rate for all the employees or create different conversion for each employee.

Loans & Leaves Monitoring

Monitor different loans and leaves of your employee and generate the history of loans and leaves report per employee.

13th Month Pay

You can define how 13th month pay is computed. Compute it annually or semi-annually. You may also adjust each employee’s 13 month computation manually.

Security & User Access

Assign each employees password and access. You may also limit the access of the payroll personnel by payroll group.

User Activity Log

Records the activity of the users.

Generate Periodic Reports

You may print by payroll period, month, quarterly or year-to-date reports.

ATM Link

The program can generate several bank format for easy submission to the bank. NO RE-ENCODING NEEDED.

Disk & Forms

Generate your government submission requirements with ease. A plain bond paper will do! All these reports are generated by EasyPay
