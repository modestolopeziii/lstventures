About LST




Our Vision

As businesses grow more competitive each year, managing and analyzing the ever-increasing flow of information is one of the crucial challenges in business today. As technology improves so does our way of doing business. Efficient & Effective tools are essential to keep up with the fast-paced electronic age. It is the vision of Lee Systems Technology Ventures to be a competitive global business solutions provider, providing efficient and effective business solutions worldwide.


Our Mission

To provide solutions through quality software to help our clients manage their business.


Background

Lee Systems Technology Ventures started as LST Ventures with 3 programmers in 1993. Providing software to clients with customized requirements. From the beginning, the company focuses on Database Programming using Clipper as programming Language and DBASE as the backend running in DOS operating system. The initial software requirements were customized sales module, payroll system, inven- tory system, purchase module, each tailored to the specific needs of our clients. Customized programs includes the varied industry of shipping, manufacturing, cement, brokerage, building management and customer information. LST Ventures later became Lee Systems Technology Ventures.
